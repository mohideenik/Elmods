What is Elmods?

Elmods is a tool that creates schedules based on your preferences. Select your desired timeslots above, and let Elmods figure out the best way to fit in your modules.

Acknowledgements

This project would not have been possible without NUSMods. In fact, the back-end of Elmods uses NUSMods' software to draw data. Elmods then works on the data to create the perfect timetable for you.

What's up with the name?

Contrary to popular belief, the word Elmods is not inspired by Elmo of Sesame Street fame. Instead, it's a portmanteau of the words Elm and Mods. The word Mods should be self-explanatory, and Elm is the programming language behind this website.
